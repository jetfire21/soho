Bio

Fernanda Romero will next be seen in Paramount’s Ghost Team One, set for release in October 2013. Next up for the Latin beauty will be Mission Park, opposite Vivica A. Fox, set for release by AMC theaters September 6th in Los Angeles, New York, Chicago and San Antonio.

Born in Mexico City, Fernanda began her singing career as part of the sensational teen-pop group Fryzzby, signed under BMG. Not long after finishing her studies, she had the opportunity to host the popular show “Control” on Univision. Shortly thereafter, Telemundo booked her on “La Ley del Silencio.” From here she went on to do feature films, starting with the independents CREATURE OF DARKNESS, a horror film with Matthew Lawrence (THE HOT CHICK), and the comedies WANNA BE ME with Shalim Ortiz (“Heroes”) and READY OR NOT with Alex Rocco (SMOKIN’ ACES).

The film that took her career to the next level was THE EYE with Jessica Alba. This opened the door for her in Hollywood, and led her to be cast in big budget films like THE BURNING PLAIN opposite Charlize Theron, DRAG ME TO HELL from director Sam Raimi, RED CANVAS with Maria Conchita Alonso, ONCE FALLEN with Ed Harris and WITHOUT MEN opposite Eva Longoria and Christian Slater.. In 2009 Fernanda participated in her first telenovela in Mexico, playing the female lead in “Eternamente Tuya” (TV Azteca). In the spring of 2011 she starred in the first bilingual series on American television, “RPM Miami”, a Telemundo Studios production that aired on NBC’s channel, mun2.
